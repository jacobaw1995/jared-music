About Jared

Jared is a country rock and traditional country singer/songwriter from Greenville, OH, and the surrounding areas. With influences like Dwight Yoakam, George Strait, and Jason Aldean, he crafts songs that blend gritty twang, heartfelt storytelling, and a touch of modern swagger.
